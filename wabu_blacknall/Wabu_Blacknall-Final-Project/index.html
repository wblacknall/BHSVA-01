Get A Job worth having

This website is used to help connect teens with jobs. In all my experience working, and for my age I have had a lot, I've realized that a lot of employers don't like having teens under their staff. They feel as though teens are too young and don't won't take their job seriously. I want to do something about it, so I made this website in order to help connect teens with employers willing to employ teens.

A huge thanks to Death to the Stock Photo for allowing us to use the beautiful photos that make this template really come to life. When using this template, make sure your photos are decent. Also make sure that the file size on your photos is kept to a minumum to keep load times to a minimum.
